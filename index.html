About Me

  • Hello, my name is Lauren Goss. I am from Hickory, NC and currently reside here. I received my Bachelors of Science in Animal Science and graduated Summa Cum Laude from North Carolina State University in May 2020. My original goal was to go to veterinary school and obtain my DVM. After realizing that this was no longer my dream, I decided to teach myself to code.

  • My career goal is to become an iOS app developer, so I began learning Swift in Summer of 2020.
